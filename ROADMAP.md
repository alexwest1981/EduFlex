# 🚀 EduFlex Roadmap: Vision 2026

**Goal:** Pilot-ready for 3–5 Swedish municipalities/universities by June 2026, focusing on integrations, support, and security.

---

## 🛠️ Current State (v3.9.4)
- **AI Accessibility Engine**: Automated EN 301 549 (DOS-lagen) validation integrated into the editor.
- **Turbo-Load Architecture**: 80% faster initial paint via route splitting and lazy-i18n.
- **Zero-Trust AI Pipeline**: PII masking and local response restoration (Schrems II-ready).
- **BankID Integration**: Secure authentication with SSN mapping.
- **Repository Hardened**: Non-root containers and sanitized credentials.

---

## 📅 Q2 2026: Scalar & Industrialization

### 🚀 High Priority: Microservice Extraction & Compliance
- **Accessibility (DOS-lagen) [DONE v3.9.4]**: Automated WCAG validation using the new machine-readable EN 301 549 dataset.
- **SCORM & xAPI Engine**: Moving the heavy I/O of e-learning packages to a dedicated worker.
- **JobEd Connect Hub**: Real-time matching between educational goals (SUSA-navet) and labor market competencies.
- **Real-time Notifications**: Dedicated service for WebSockets/SSE to handle 10,000+ parallel connections.
- **Integration Hub**: Asynchronous batch syncing for Skolverket, Ladok, and HR systems.

### 🛡️ Security & Scalability
- **ISO 27001 Compliance**: Further hardening of encryption at rest for all PII.
- **Enterprise IAM**: Deep integration with Azure AD and OKTA beyond standard OIDC.

---

## 🧠 Q3 2026: AI Agentic Learning

### 🤖 Generative Everything
- **AF MCP AI Engine**: Leveraging the Arbetsförmedlingen MCP Server to accelerate development of matching and taxonomy analysis.
- **AI Tutors 3.0**: Proactive coaches that anticipate student gaps *before* they fail a quiz.
- **Adaptive Curriculum**: Real-time generation of custom learning paths based on VAK (Visual, Auditory, Kinesthetic) profiles.

### 📊 Advanced ROI
- **Skills Gap Heatmaps**: Organizational visualization of competence gaps compared to market demand (JobTech integration).
- **Mastery Scoring**: SM-2 based Spaced Repetition integrated across all course types.

---

## 🌐 Vision: The Global LLP
EduFlex aims to be the leading Learning Lifecycle Platform, transforming from a traditional LMS into a continuous growth ecosystem for Students, Teachers, and Principals alike.
