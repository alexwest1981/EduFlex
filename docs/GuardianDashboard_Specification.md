# Specification: EduFlex Guardian Dashboard (Vårdnadshavardashboard)

The Guardian Dashboard is designed to be a "Mission Control for Parents." It prioritizes immediate, actionable information about their child's school life without administrative overhead.

## 1. Top Section - Quick Insight (Snabb överblick)
- **Child Selector**: Dropdown or avatar-row to switch between children (if multiple).
- **Daily Snapshot Card**:
  - Today's classes/schedule.
  - Attendance status (Checked-in / Reported absence).
  - Next big event (Test, development talk, field trip).
- **Notification Row**: Ticker or icons for highlights:
  - New messages.
  - Recent grades/assessments.
  - Documents pending signature/acknowledgment.

## 2. Central Modules (Kärnboxar)

### To-Do & Upcoming (Att göra / Kommande)
- **Assignments & Tests**: List of homework, upcoming tests, and projects.
- **Status Indicators**: Not started, In progress, Submitted, Graded.
- **Filter**: Filter by type (Homework, Test, Project).

### Attendance & Absence (Närvaro och frånvaro)
- **Real-time Status**: In school, Late, Absent.
- **History View**: Weekly/Monthly percentage bars.
- **Quick Action**: "Report Absence" button (Today/Tomorrow, Full/Partial day).

### Results & Development (Resultat och utveckling)
- **Subject Overview**: Latest grades and teacher comments per subject.
- **Trend Indicator**: Simple visual (Up/Down) comparing to previous periods.
- **At-Risk Indicator**: Highlight subjects where the student is at risk of failing.

### Communication (Kommunikation)
- **Message Hub**: Conversations with Mentors, Teachers, Admin, and Health Team.
- **Unread Counters**: Clearly visible counters for unanswered messages.
- **Quick Contact Buttons**: "Contact Mentor", "Contact Admin", "Contact Health Team".

### News & Information (Nyheter och information)
- **Feeds**: School-wide news, Class-specific updates, Mentor/Principal letters.
- **Level Filter**: Filter by School, Class, or Preschool/After-school.

### Schedule & Calendar (Schema och kalender)
- **Daily View**: Timeline of classes with times and teacher names.
- **Calendar**: Weekly view including homework deadlines, tests, holidays, and events.
- **Export**: Integration with Google/Outlook/iCal.

## 3. Sub-Sections & Extra Panels

### Development Talks (Utvecklingssamtal)
- **Booking**: Interactive calendar to pick/change meeting times.
- **History**: Notes and summaries from the latest talks (if shared by mentor).

### Preschool/After-school (Fritids/Förskola)
- **Weekly Schedule**: Edit and view child's pickup/drop-off times.
- **Notes**: "Picking up by grandmother today", etc.

### Portfolio & Learning (Elevarbete)
- **Gallery**: Photos, projects, and work shared by teachers.
- **Interaction**: Optional "Positive Comment" (Heart/Like) interaction.

### Documents & Consents (Dokument & Samtycken)
- **Pending Actions**: Digital signing of agreements, policies, and excursion consents.
- **Archive**: History of signed documents.

### Contact & Directory (Kontakt och uppgifter)
- **Class List**: Contact info for other parents (subject to consent) and staff.
- **Profile Management**: Update own contact details, language preferences, and notification settings.

### Fees & Payments (Avgifter och betalningar)
- **Financial Overview**: View school fees, lunch costs, excursions, etc.
- **Payment Integration**: Link to pay or view receipts.

## 4. Premium Details

### Mood Tracker (Barnets känsla)
- **Self-Assessment**: Display child's self-reported mood/well-being (if enabled).

### Notification Settings
- **Granularity**: Choose between SMS, Email, or Push.
- **Frequency**: Daily vs. Weekly summaries.

### Accessibility
- **Language Support**: Multi-language support (SV, EN, etc.).
- **Visuals**: Dark Mode support and Font-size adjustments.

### Support
- **Help Center**: Quick "Need Help?" link with guides and support contact.
